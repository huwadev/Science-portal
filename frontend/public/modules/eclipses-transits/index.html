References, Credits & Scientific Attributions

1. Astronomical Ephemerides & Calculations

All high-precision solar, lunar, and planetary transit calculations, Delta-T time corrections, and celestial coordinate transformations are powered by Astronomy Engine.

Cross, D. (2024). Astronomy Engine: A C# / C++ / JavaScript / Python library for solar system ephemerides. MIT License.

2. NASA Five Millennium Catalog of Eclipses

Eclipse canonical predictions and century catalog benchmarks derived from the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center 5-Millennium Canon of Solar & Lunar Eclipses.

Espenak, F., & Meeus, J. (2009). Five Millennium Catalog of Solar Eclipses: -1999 to +3000 (2000 BCE to 3000 CE). NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, NASA TP-2009-214172.

3. Geographic Mapping & 3D WebGL Engines

2D interactive map tiles provided by CartoDB Positron Dark Basemap © OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL / CC BY 3.0).
